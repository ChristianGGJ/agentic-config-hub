# Project Brief: Terra & Thread - Handmade Ceramics Online Store

## The idea

We want to launch an online store selling our handmade ceramics - mugs,
bowls, and vases - directly to customers across Europe. Today we sell only
at weekend craft markets; the store lets us reach buyers year-round.

## What we have planned

- A product catalog with studio photography for around 80 items
- A brand site built on a hosted storefront platform
- Instagram and Pinterest marketing to drive traffic to the store
- Shoppers can create customer accounts and sign up for our email
  newsletter for restock announcements
- Seasonal collections and limited drops to keep the catalog fresh
- Pricing in euros, with a launch discount for market regulars

## Goals for year one

- 500 orders in the first year
- Grow the newsletter to 2,000 subscribers
- Keep the studio's weekend market presence alongside the store
